My Story

"As a young Turkish perfume expert, my journey has always been driven by an endless obsession with liquid emotion. To capture the absolute essence of luxury, I traveled across continents, exploring hidden corners of the world to source the rarest, most uncompromising raw ingredients. My heritage taught me that scent is a bridge between time, memory, and heritage."

"At the core of Extrette lies my ultimate creation: Kingdom. For me, Kingdom is not just a perfume; it is a gateway to the majestic, echoes of ancient palaces and imperial courts from lost empires. Every note is structured to evoke the heavy, golden aura of historic thrones and sacred stone walls. This brand is a manifest of my passion, designed for those who seek to rule their own presence."

Efe Erol Founder & Perfume Expert
