My Story

"As a young Turkish perfume expert, my journey has always been driven by an endless obsession with liquid emotion. To capture the absolute essence of luxury, I traveled across continents, exploring hidden corners of the world to source the rarest, most uncompromising raw ingredients. My heritage taught me that scent is a bridge between time, memory, and heritage."

"At the core of Extrette lies my ultimate creation: Kingdom. For me, Kingdom is not just a perfume; it is a gateway to the majestic, echoes of ancient palaces and imperial courts from lost empires.
